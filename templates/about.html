About Shikshya

Shikshya is an advanced, role-based, end-to-end educational portal created to deliver high-quality, self-paced learning to students across Nepal. Built with a highly responsive glassmorphic aesthetic and powered by robust Python systems, our platform brings academic rigor and professional tech training directly to your screen.

Our Mission

To democratize learning by building accessible, secure, and production-ready toolsets. We connect professional mentors with eager students, equipping them with the quizzes, resources, assignments, and verified certificates necessary to launch and excel in tech careers.

Core Features

Immersive Video Learning

Resume exactly where you left off. Watch lessons with multi-speed controls, take inline notes, and track your completions.

Assignments & Timed Quizzes

Take timer-locked exams, check your answers instantly, submit files, and receive rubric-based teacher reviews.

Secure QR Certificates

Complete all lessons in a course, and instantly download a certificate embedded with a unique verification code and QR code.

Collaborative Q&A

Engage in Q&A course discussion forums, interact directly with teachers, and consult our AI chat tutor 24/7.
